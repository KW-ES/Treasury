FTP

Funds Transfer Pricing

True Cost of Money & Proper Allocations

Mechanism[1]

  • FTP is a process:
    • Consolidating bank's funding requirements
      • Results from provisioning of bank's products in central Treasury book
      • This enables Treasury to efficiently control and manage the bank's funding and Maturity Mismatch
    • Prices funding costs/benefits of a banks products or business lines
      • to establish true profitability of these parts

Usage in Treasury

Treasury & FTP

Source [1]
  • All funds needed for banks requirements (assets) are obtained from deposits (liabilities) from Treasury via an internal loan/deposit or funding transaction
  • Treasury manages price via Liquidity Premium Curve
    • Prices these transactions on top of Interbank Swap Rate or Curve
    • See Funding Curve Diagram
    • Together → FTP Rate
  • Treasury maintains the different Premiums for different products to indicate their specific impact.
  • At Minimum, Treasury needs to maintain a positive LP spread between the FTP rate paid by Treasury for funds provided by liability products.
    • Surplus from the spread allows for the cost of the banks funding and Maturity Mismatch on the financial market to be managed

Cap & Floor

  • FTP Rate for Liabilities → Cap Rate for Client Deposits
    • To profit, the branch needs to pay the client a lower rate on their deposit
  • FTP Rate for Assets → Floor Rate for Client Loans
    • To profit, the branch must charge the client a higher rate on their loan

Relationship to Repo Sweeping

🔹 Relation to FTP

  • Repo funding trades are internalised via mirror deals between Repo Desk and Trading Desk.
  • Repo Desk’s external trades are booked against GTSY portfolios.
  • This allows Treasury to allocate true FTP cost of repo funding back to desks.

FTP Requirements

Across Business Units

There is no differentiation in FTP pricing across RMB business units, a central GTSY administered FTP Curve framework governs all portfolios in Murex, creating automation of liquidity pricing

  • Trade Funding Process
    • Real-time, 1-to-1 Matched Maturity funding process
    • System configured via Portfolio business rules to automatically generate the requisite internal funding transaction with matching Cashflow profile and maturity at an FTP rate corresponding to the underlying transaction maturity
  • GM Trading Portfolios
    • FX
    • Fixed Income
    • Equities
    • Commodities
    • Secured Funding Desk
    • Money Market Desk
  • IBD
    • Portfolios with Term Funded or structured assets
  • TTS
    • Portfolios with cash or Liquidity Pool exposures
